This is a social party game where players try to identify the impostor.

Each round, most players receive the same word, while one or more players are impostors and do not know the word.

Players take turns saying related words or hints to prove they know the secret word—without making it too obvious.

The impostor must listen carefully, adapt, and blend in without ever knowing the actual word.

After discussion, players try to vote out the impostor.
